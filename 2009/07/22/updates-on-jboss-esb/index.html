Updates on JBoss ESB.

Date: 22 - 07 - 2009

The JBoss ESB team rolled out its 4.6 release yesterday, one of its main feature that it is introduced is to support the JBoss AS5, so go and grab it to try it out.

Also spotted that Len has composed three great posts on JBoss SOA-P, it applies also to the JBoss ESB. 1. SOA Platform Gateways and Notifiers. This talks about the Gateway, ESB aware message, ESB non-aware message, Notifier concepts that from the JBoss ESB.

  1. When The Content knows the way. This talks about the JBoss ESB integrates with Drools.

  2. Hanging Together on the SOA Platform - Introduction to the ESB-jBPM integration. This entry talks about the ESB and jBPM integration.

One thing that I must say is that these three entries are very well written, easy to understand. Very good work, Len. ;-)

If you want to read more Len's posts, then follow the soa-p's blog.
